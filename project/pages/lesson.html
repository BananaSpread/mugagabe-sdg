1.1 - Introduction

Sustainable Development Goal 13 (SDG 13) addresses the urgent need to combat climate change and its effects on the planet. Recognizing climate change as one of the most pressing global challenges, this goal emphasizes the importance of immediate action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and enhance resilience against climate-related hazards.

SDG 13 is integral to achieving sustainable development, as climate change impacts various aspects of human life, including health, food security, and economic stability. By fostering international cooperation, promoting innovative solutions, and mobilizing resources, SDG 13 aims to limit global warming, protect vulnerable communities, and ensure a sustainable future for all.

Through education, awareness, and collaborative efforts, SDG 13 seeks to empower individuals, communities, and nations to take meaningful action against climate change and build a more sustainable and resilient world.
